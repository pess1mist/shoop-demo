### 工作流表结构关键点
```sql
-- workflow_instance关键字段
process_id (bigint, NOT NULL) - 关联workflow_definition.id
process_key (varchar) - 流程标识
status (varchar) - RUNNING/COMPLETED/PENDING/REJECTED

-- workflow_task关键字段  
instance_id (bigint) - 关联workflow_instance.id
node_key (varchar) - 节点标识
status (varchar) - PENDING/COMPLETED
action (varchar) - APPROVE/REJECT/SUBMIT/HANDLE/CONFIRM
```

### 采购表结构关键点
```sql
-- purchase_plan关键字段
plan_code (varchar) - 计划编号
dept_code (varchar) - 部门代码
status (varchar) - PENDING/APPROVED/IN_PROGRESS/COMPLETED

-- purchase_order关键字段
order_id (bigint, AUTO_INCREMENT) - 自增主键
approval_status (varchar) - PENDING/APPROVED
```
